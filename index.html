Workshop on Trustworthy Artificial Intelligence


Join us for a groundbreaking event focused on Trustworthy AI! Artificial intelligence (AI) is reshaping our world, empowering organizations to enhance forecasting, innovate products and services, and boost efficiency. Yet, concerns over AI's reliability prompt crucial discussions on ensuring its trustworthiness.

Our Trustworthy AI Day, hosted by Confiance.ai and Confiance IA alongside industry and academic partners, aims to address these concerns head-on. Delving into the technological and regulatory dimensions, our event offers a platform to exchange insights, experiences, and ideas on fostering responsible AI practices.

This day is dedicated to exploring sustainable, ethical, and secure AI, inviting participants to connect with key stakeholders and foster collaboration. Whether you're passionate about AI ethics or eager to navigate its complexities, join us for a day of enlightenment and collaboration. All enthusiasts of Trustworthy AI are warmly welcomed.

Stay tuned for updates!

About Us: Confiance.ai





Confiance.ai is a unique French community dedicated to the design and industrialization of trustworthy critical systems based on artificial intelligence. Confiance.ai aims to help industrials integrate trustworthy AI in their critical systems thanks to an environment built with methods and tools that can be integrated in any engineering workshop.

About Us: Confiance IA





Confiance IA is a Quebec industrial research consortium working to develop sustainable, ethical, safe and responsible artificial intelligence. Confiance IA brings together private and public actors to co-develop methods and tools to generate concrete solutions to the challenges linked to the industrialization of AI.
